Welcome To Our Institution

Hattaraki Medical Institute and Research Center, Gadhinglaj will seek to be a center of excellence in Medical Education, Research, and HealthCare services at the national level.The institute in pursuit of its vision will provide outstanding educational experience, in all the disciplines of Medicine and allied Health Sciences.

The self contained campus has excellent infrastructure and learning resources that collectively provide a stimulating and enjoyable learning ambience. Student amenities are within the campus, viz. banks, ATMs, Post office, indoor and out door stadiam, auditorium, garden, prayer places, book shop, canteens, etc. The campus is wi-fi, eco-friendly being, mobile free, vehicle free and plastic free. To understand the complexities of and to keep pace with the advances in medical science, experiments and case studies need to be conducted in well equipped laboratories. The College has an array of modern laboratories with state-of the art equipments. Trained staff provide assistance and guidance to the students.

This is a unique facility to train the students using simulation technology. They get hands on opportunity to perform clinical procedures on models, manikins, tissues and simulators. The lab conducts more than sixty workshops in a year for students of Hattarki institution. Behind the reputation of the college, are the solid credentials of an accomplished and experienced faculty imparting quality education through a well-planned curriculum and structured courses.

The college encourages the students association to organise several cultural, literary and sports activities under the guidance of the faculty. Spiritual leaders are invited to deliver motivating talks. Popular and National Festivals are celebrated. Thus the college provides full opportunity to facilitate overall and holistic development of the students into culturally sensitive and socially responsible, good citizens which is as important as graduating from a medical college.
