THEORY PAPER 2-PRINCIPLES AND PRACTISE OF THEATRE MANAGEMENT AND MATERIAL

MANAGEMENT OF THEATRE
1.Need and why OT planning is required.
2. Structural designing and planning of OT in 20 bedded up to 100 bedded hospitals.
3. Planning and strategic analysis of activities and their implementation.
4. Organizing,co-ordinating and controlling.
5. Interpersonal relation and communication(Obstacle and improvement of the same oral and written).
6. Dynamics of Group behaviour-conflicts and solutions with motivations.
7. Staff patient relationship
8. Organising with theatre staff.
9. Zoning and circulation of OT.
10. Patient care Motivation guidance / psychological support.
11. Patient escort to and from the OT.
12. Communication and Interacting with General ward Staff about O.T. procedures,
information / Guidance about treatment etc
13.Care of O.T before surgery /during surgery and after surgery.
14. Record keeping / Documentation-(Both-of No of surgery / patient and no of instruments, equipments etc)and O.T.staff records.
15. Principles of Material Management- Defination / scope.Functional Objectives.
16. Integrated Material Management Aims;Advantages,problems with hospital etc.
17. Material planning-Indenting guidelines,materials classification.
18. Inventory Control -Safety storage ABC and VED analysis.
19. Utilization of O T-Stores handling and management etc.
20. Remedial measures:Drugs reviewing,control of pilferage and wastage.
21. Detailed documentation of present material,cost charting equipment cost etc.
22. Classification of used and unused material before and after surgery and its planning prior
to surgery.
23. Consumable and non consumable store,utilization and documentation.
24. Baseline stock and sysytems for billing of operation theatre material
25. Control/Record of all medical / surgical material and how to demand as and when required.
