Documents Required

The candidates should produce or attach following certificate along with his application form during admission:
1)10th pass.
2)School Leaving Certificate.
3)10th Board Certificate.
4)Domicile,Nationality certificate.
5)Pass-port size four photographs
6)Attempt Certificate of 10th pass.
7)Migration Certificate is required compulsary for the candidates outside Maharashtra state
8)Candidate has to produce all originals,documents at the time of interview

Examination

I)FIRST SEMESTER-I(JULY TO DECEMBER)
Unit Test-One Compulsary.
Tutorial-Two Compulsary.
Terminal Exam(Oral/Theory/Practical Exam)
Note:-(1st Semister Exam will be conducted at college at level)

II)SECOND SEMISTER-II(JANUARY TO JULY)
Unit Test-One Compulsary.
Tutorial-Two Compulsary.
Priliminary Examination
Annual Examination-Theory/Practical/Oral exam will be conducted by Board Exam M.S.B.V.E. Mumbai.

(Note:One's fees paid is not refundable).

Dress Code

Library

Attendance

Attendance is compulsary for all students.Any one who fails to attend 50% of the total number of classes held in the year for each subject shall not be permitted to fill up his/her Board examination form.
